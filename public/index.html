Maleficent

Once a kind-hearted fairy, she became a powerful and vengeful figure after betrayal. Her dark powers, fueled by pain, led her to curse Aurora, but her journey toward forgiveness ultimately defines her legacy as protector of the Moors.

Aurora

The princess cursed by Maleficent grows up unaware of her true heritage. With courage and kindness, she unites the realms of men and magic, becoming a symbol of peace, hope, and the bridge between two worlds.

Diaval

Maleficent's loyal shape-shifting raven and closest confidant. His unwavering friendship and sacrifice help Maleficent rediscover love and compassion, proving that loyalty can heal even the darkest heart.

Stefan

Once Maleficent's greatest friend, his ambition turns him into her deadliest enemy. Haunted by guilt and fear, his choices set in motion a curse that will echo through both kingdoms forever.

ANCIENT CHRONICLES

Maleficent's Journey

From a young fairy full of love to a formidable sorceress driven by pain, her evolution is a testament to the complex nature of good and evil. The wings of betrayal gave rise to the horns of vengeance.

Heart of a Villain

At first glance, Maleficent may seem like a typical villain, but her true motivations are more complex. Her actions stem from deep betrayal, unyielding love for the Moors, and a fierce desire for justice.

Maleficent's Influence

In a world of clear-cut good vs. evil, Maleficent breaks the mold by redefining the villain archetype. Her complexity has inspired generations of storytellers to embrace moral ambiguity and dark femininity.

Maleficent's Enemies

Beyond the curse on Aurora lies a darker history of those who shaped her view of the world. Not all enemies wear crowns — some once wore the mask of friendship.
